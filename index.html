Hi, I’m Aga.

Welcome to my blog.

I’ve been experiencing a sharp pain on the left side of my body for five years.

After extensive research, I identified its root cause and I’m actively working to resolve it. This journal documents:

  • My process of fixing chronic tension pain
  • Physical tensions and headaches analyzed through mental and emotional lenses
  • Adjustments to my worldview and habits

I’m also exercising my eyes to improve my vision (L −1.00 D, R −3.25 D myopia; intermittent astigmatism). These exercises surprisingly help correct postural issues and build muscle strength. Best effects are when they're combined with Pilates, massage and physiotherapy. And a decent diet.
